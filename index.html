Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last updated: April 27, 2026

About This Service

ORCA is a proprietary algorithmic options trading alert system operated exclusively by its owner (a single individual operating under the Bauthaus business entity). The system sends automated SMS notifications — including trade alerts, profit target notices, expiration warnings, and position risk alerts — to the registered operator's mobile number only.

Who Receives Messages

ORCA is a single-recipient system. Only the business owner/operator receives messages. No third-party end users, customers, or subscribers are ever enrolled.

How Opt-In Consent Is Collected

The operator explicitly opts in to receive ORCA SMS alerts by entering their own mobile phone number into the system configuration file (environment variable TWILIO_TO_NUMBER) during system setup. This self-directed configuration step constitutes the operator's opt-in consent. Consent is not shared, transferred, or extended to any other individual or entity.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on market activity and system events. The operator may receive multiple messages per day during active trading sessions, or no messages outside of market hours.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please check with your mobile carrier regarding your SMS plan.

How to Opt Out

The operator may opt out of ORCA SMS alerts at any time using either of the following methods:

  • Reply STOP to any ORCA message to unsubscribe immediately.
  • Remove the mobile number from the ORCA system configuration file.

After opting out via STOP, no further messages will be sent until the number is re-enrolled.

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any ORCA message for assistance, or contact the operator directly at dandersen@bauthaus.com.

Data Privacy & Mobile Number Sharing

Mobile phone numbers collected by ORCA are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. The mobile number is used solely to deliver operational alerts to the system operator. No data is sold, rented, or disclosed to any outside party.

Data We Collect

  • The operator's mobile phone number (stored in system configuration only)
  • Message delivery logs retained by Twilio per their standard platform terms

Third-Party Services

ORCA uses Twilio to deliver SMS messages. Message delivery is subject to Twilio's platform terms and carrier policies. ORCA does not control carrier routing or delivery confirmation.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in system functionality or regulatory requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
